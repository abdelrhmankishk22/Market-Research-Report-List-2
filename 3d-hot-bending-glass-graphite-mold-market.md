<p><p>The 3D hot bending glass graphite mold market offers three types of molds: solid mold, hollow mold, and frame mold. Solid molds are made of a continuous graphite block and are suitable for producing glass products with complex shapes. Hollow molds have an empty space inside and are used for shaping hollow glass products like bottles and bulbs. Frame molds consist of a graphite frame and are ideal for creating flat glass products such as mirrors and windows. Each type of mold caters to specific glass shaping requirements.</p></p>
