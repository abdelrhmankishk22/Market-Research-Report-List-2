<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) composites are materials made from a combination of fibers and polymer resins. The fibers, typically made of glass, carbon, or aramid, provide strength and stiffness, while the polymer resins bind the fibers together and provide a matrix for them. FRP composites offer a range of advantages over traditional materials like steel and concrete, including high strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and design flexibility.</p><p>The current outlook for the FRP composites market is optimistic.
