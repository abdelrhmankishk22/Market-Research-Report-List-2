There are two main types of digital temperature sensors available in the market: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors come into direct physical contact with the object being measured, providing accurate and reliable temperature readings. On the other hand, non-contact type sensors use infrared technology to measure temperature without any physical contact, making them suitable for applications where contact may be challenging or undesirable. Both types of sensors offer unique benefits and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
